Philadelphia has a rich tradition of Orthodox Christian expression. There are dozens of parishes in the Greater Metropolitan Area, and many other groups hosting events, if you know where to look.

Former iterations of this website aimed to educate about all the parishes and events going on in the area, but we've reduced our ambitions since then. If you have any questions, comments, or concerns, please send an email to info@orthodoxphilly.org, and we'll see if there's anything we can do to help.

For those seeking a parish community or other group in the area, we can recommend the following resources:

  • The Assembly of Canonical Orthodox Bishops of America Parish Locator
  • The Orthodoxy in America Parish Locator
  • The Standing Conference of Oriental Orthodox Churches Parish Locator (this one was not functioning last I tried it)
  • The Philadelphia Pan-Orthodox (Young Adult) Facebook Group
  • The Orthodox Philly Facebook Group
  • Ancient Faith Ministries (for general information purposes)
